# Standard KYC income verification

## Overview
Incomplete income evidence should be routed to human approval before onboarding proceeds.

## Evidence requirements
- Recent salary slips, payslips, or payroll statements.
- Proof of income or source-of-funds evidence when the customer profile requires it.
- Reviewer confirmation if the supplied documents do not clearly match the declared profile.

## Decision guidance
The workflow should not auto-approve when income support is partial or ambiguous. A reviewer should confirm whether the submitted evidence is sufficient before the case can be closed.
