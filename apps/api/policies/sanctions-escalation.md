# Sanctions escalation workflow

## Overview
Potential sanctions matches must be escalated to the review board with supporting context.

## Screening requirements
- Screen all beneficial owners and counterparties before any recommendation is finalized.
- Capture the exact matching fields and the screening source used to produce the alert.
- Preserve the supporting context for reviewer comparison.

## Decision guidance
Any plausible sanctions hit should move to the escalation queue rather than being auto-cleared. A human reviewer must assess the evidence and document the final disposition.
