# Standard KYC onboarding policy

## Overview
Approve when identity and address evidence are complete and the applicant does not trigger any escalation flags.

## Evidence requirements
- Passport or other government-issued identity proof.
- A recent proof-of-address document such as a utility bill.
- Supporting income evidence when the workflow requires affordability validation.

## Decision guidance
This policy should support fast approval for straightforward onboarding cases. If any required document is missing or inconsistent, route the case to a reviewer instead of forcing an automatic decision.
