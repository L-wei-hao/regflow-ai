# Enhanced due diligence standard

## Overview
Cases with incomplete source-of-funds evidence should remain in human review until the supporting documents are verified.

## Evidence requirements
- Supporting documents that explain the source of funds or wealth.
- Secondary evidence that confirms the applicant’s claimed activity profile.
- Additional reviewer notes whenever the risk posture is elevated.

## Decision guidance
Enhanced due diligence cases should default to review when documents are incomplete, contradictory, or too generic to support a safe recommendation. The rule is to be conservative and leave an audit-friendly explanation.
